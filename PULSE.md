# The Yard Keepers — Business Pulse (Marketing Playbook)

Mark runs marketing (top of funnel: get found → get leads). Nyte runs operations
(turn leads into recurring customers). This is how we read the pulse.

---

## The 5 vital signs

| Sign | Question | Where to look | Goal |
|------|----------|---------------|------|
| **Leads** | How many quote requests came in? | Formspree dashboard (you're BCC'd on each) | grow weekly |
| **Traffic + source** | Is marketing driving visits, from where? | Vercel Analytics / GA4 | grow weekly |
| **Conversion rate** | Do visitors actually request a quote? | GA4: `quote_submitted` ÷ visitors | **8%+** |
| **Local discovery** | Found for "dog waste removal Florence"? | Search Console + Google Business Profile | climbing |
| **Health (the real one)** | Recurring subscribers + monthly revenue | Pulse sheet (Nyte updates) | grow monthly |

---

## Monday pulse — 5 minutes

1. **Leads** — count new Formspree submissions since last Monday. Up or down?
2. **Traffic** — Vercel Analytics: visitors this week + top source (Google? social? direct?).
3. **Conversion** — GA4: `quote_submitted` events ÷ visitors. Under 8%? The page or the
   traffic quality needs work. Over 8%? Pour more into whatever channel is sending traffic.
4. **Calls** — GA4: `call_click` events (nav/form/footer/mobile). Phone-first leads.
5. **Search** — Search Console: impressions & avg position for the Florence keywords.

Jot the five numbers in the Pulse sheet so trends are visible over time.

## Monthly review — 20 minutes

- **Recurring subscribers**: net new this month (the health metric — recurring revenue
  is the whole game for this business).
- **Lead → customer rate**: of last month's leads, how many became paying clients?
- **Cost per lead** (if running ads): ad spend ÷ leads.
- **Top converting source**: double down on it next month.
- **August check**: as the Corvallis launch nears, watch for Corvallis search impressions.

---

## What each tool tells you

- **Vercel Web Analytics** — fast, cookieless visitor + source counts. The daily glance.
- **Google Analytics 4** — deep marketing: channels, funnels, the conversion events
  (`quote_submitted`, `call_click`). The weekly analysis.
- **Google Search Console** — what people search to find you, and your ranking. The SEO pulse.
- **Google Business Profile** — Maps + local 3-pack. For a local service this often drives
  more calls than the website. Check its built-in insights weekly.
- **Formspree** — the lead inbox + a record of every submission.

---

## Conversion levers (when the rate is low)

- Add real customer **reviews/testimonials** (collect from first clients ASAP).
- Add **before/after** yard photos.
- Tighten the hero promise / CTA.
- Make sure the **phone** is answered fast (PRD: respond within hours).
- Push the **Google Business Profile** — reviews there lift both calls and ranking.
